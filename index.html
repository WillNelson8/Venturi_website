Everything You Need to Stay Airworthy

Venturi brings modern software design to aircraft management, helping you track maintenance, log flights, and stay compliant.

Maintenance Tracking

Never miss an inspection. Track annuals, 100-hour inspections, ADs, and squawks. Get reminded when maintenance is due based on hours or calendar dates.

Digital Logbook

Log flights with ease. Track hours, routes, and categories. View your logbook as a list or calendar. Export to CSV or PDF whenever you need.

Insights & Analytics

Visualize your flying patterns. See flight time by year, aircraft, and category. Beautiful charts help you understand your aviation journey.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. No cloud sync required. No accounts. No subscriptions. Just a clean, private tool for aircraft owners.

FAA Integration

Enter your tail number and we'll pull aircraft details directly from the FAA registry. Set up your aircraft in seconds.

iPad Optimized

Designed specifically for iPad. Large, tappable controls. Split-view layouts. Perfect for the cockpit or the hangar.
