From cost splits between co-owners to forecasting your next annual—Venturi brings clarity to the financial side of aviation.

Cost Splits for Co-Owners

Share aircraft costs transparently with your co-ownership group. Track everyone's hours, expenses, and contributions in real time—so reconciliations are quick and trust stays high.

Maintenance Records

Log every inspection, squawk, and AD in one organized place. Get reminded before anything is due, whether it's based on hours or the calendar.

Digital Flight Logbook

Log flights in seconds. Hours, routes, categories, pilots—all tracked and ready to view as a list, calendar, or export to CSV or PDF on demand.

See exactly what every flight costs. Break down expenses by aircraft, category, and time period with charts that make the financial picture obvious at a glance.

Project your aircraft's expenses over the next month, quarter, or year. Plan for annuals, overhauls, and reserve contributions with confidence.
